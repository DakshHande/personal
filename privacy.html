Privacy Policy

At Daksh Hande's official website, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data and ensure your experience on our site is safe and enjoyable.

No User Accounts: We do not require any login or signup to access the content on our website. All eBook content is available for free, and no personal information is needed to view or download it.

No Cookies: Our website does not use cookies to track user activity. You can browse our site without any data being stored on your device.

No Third-Party Links: We do not include links to third-party websites, ensuring that your browsing experience remains entirely within our site.

No Ads: There are no advertisements displayed on our website. We aim to provide a clean and focused environment for our users.

No Subscription: There is no subscription service associated with our website, ensuring that users are not required to provide personal information or payment details.

Optional Donations: If you wish to support Daksh and his initiatives, you may do so through our support page. Donations are completely optional and not mandatory. If you choose to donate, you may do so via Daksh's personal phone number, which will be provided on the support page.

We appreciate your support, but please remember that your decision to donate is entirely voluntary. There is no obligation to contribute, and all eBook content remains free for users to access.

Thank you for visiting my website. If you have any questions regarding our Privacy Policy or wish to learn more about our initiatives, feel free to reach out via the contact page.
